# CEWF: A New Methodology for Dev-Driven Writing

## Abstract

Introduction to the Compound Engineering Writing Framework (CEWF).

## Content

The Compound Engineering Writing Framework (CEWF) is designed to bridge the gap between development and writing. By treating articles as products, developers can leverage their existing tools (like Obsidian and CLI agents) to manage the entire lifecycle of a writing piece.

### Key Stages

1. **Capture**: Record ideas as they happen.
2. **Research**: Build a knowledge base with data and logs.
3. **Develop**: Create automation scripts to handle repetitive tasks.
4. **Write**: Draft articles in a structured format.
5. **Batch**: Publish to multiple platforms with a single command.
